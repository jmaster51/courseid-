Webpage Review: Roller Coaster Data Base

  • What is the company/organization: Roller Coaster Data Base (rcdb).
  • Website url:Rcdb.com

  • Who is the intended audiance: The intended audiance is anyone who wants to learn or look up facts about a certian roller coaster.
  • Do I believe the site reaches it's intended audience: Yes, because you can look up any roller coaster in the world weather it's still oporating or torn down.
  • Was this site useful to me: Yes, because I loved roller coasters ever since I was little and whenever I find out about a new roller coaster being built I go to rcdb to find out how tall it is, how fast it goes, etc.
  • Colors/graphics that are used on the home page of this website: The colors that are used are orange, white, black, and blue. The images that are used are pictures of diffrent roller coasters and the logo.
